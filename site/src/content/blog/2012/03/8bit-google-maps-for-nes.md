Google has long neglected one of the most popular and best-selling devices ever -- but all that ends today as Google releases their first NES product: Google Maps 8-bit for NES.

The product has all the features you're used to from the desktop version, including street view:

-   search for a street or place
-   directions from one place to another
-   voice search
-   street view
-   and the ability to save routes and locations

All this is made possible by Google's cloud infrastructure.

For a quick trial, head over to [Google Maps](http://maps.google.com/ "Google Maps") and click on the quest button at the top-right of the screen.
