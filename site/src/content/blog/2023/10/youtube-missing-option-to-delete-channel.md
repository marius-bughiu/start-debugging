---
If you follow YouTube’s guides to deleting a channel, you might find that in your case the `Remove YouTube content` is missing altogether. This seems to be the case with newer accounts, but no worries, there's a workaround.

Go to **YouTube Studio** instead. Go to **Settings** > **Channel** > **Advanced settings** and scroll to the bottom. There you should find the **Remove YouTube content** option available. Click on it and follow the instructions to have your channel and all the content associated with it removed.
