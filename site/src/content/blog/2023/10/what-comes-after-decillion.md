This is quite a frequent question. You’ve got:

-   million (6 zeroes)
-   billion (9 zeroes)
-   trillion (12 zeroes)
-   quadrillion (15 zeroes)
-   quintillion (18 zeroes)
-   sextillion (21 zeroes)
-   septillion (24 zeroes)
-   octillion (27 zeroes)
-   nonillion (30 zeroes)
-   decillion (33 zeroes)

But what comes after decillion?

The answer is: **undecillion**. An undecillion, has 36 zeroes. That’s a lot of zeroes: 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000.

But there’s more. If you look past the undecillion, you’ve got:

-   duodecillion (39 zeroes)
-   tredecillion (42 zeroes)
-   quattuordecillion (45 zeroes)
-   quindecillion (48 zeroes)
-   sexdecillion (51 zeroes)
-   septendecillion (54 zeroes)
-   octodecillion (57 zeroes)
-   novemdecillion (60 zeroes)
-   vigintillion (63 zeroes)
-   centillion (303 zeroes)
